## Lego ev3 micropython (EV3Dev)

![](./images/LegoEV3.jpeg)

ev3dev is a Debian Linux-based operating system that runs on several LEGO® MINDSTORMS compatible platforms including the LEGO® MINDSTORMS EV3 and Raspberry Pi-powered BrickPi.
